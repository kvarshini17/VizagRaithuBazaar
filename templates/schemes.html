How to Apply?
  1. Visit the official website of the scheme you're interested in
  2. Read the eligibility criteria carefully
  3. Gather required documents (Aadhaar, land records, bank details, etc.)
  4. Register/Login on the portal
  5. Fill the application form with accurate details
  6. Upload documents as required
  7. Submit application and note down the reference number
  8. Track your application status on the portal
Tip: Visit your nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or agricultural office if you need help with the application process.
